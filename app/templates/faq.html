Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Hermius

General Questions

Hermius is a secure and anonymous chatroom platform that allows users to communicate without revealing their identity. It offers both anonymous chat options and account-based features for enhanced functionality.

Hermius uses end-to-end encryption to ensure that all messages are securely stored in the database. Only encrypted messages are saved, and no personal data is shared with third parties.

No, you can join chatrooms anonymously. However, creating an account unlocks additional features like modifying account details and accessing your user profile.

Registered users can:

  • Modify their account details (username, email, password).
  • Access their user profile.
  • Enjoy a more personalized experience.

Chatroom Questions

You can create a chatroom by clicking the "Create a Room" button on the homepage. A unique room code will be generated for you.

To join a chatroom, enter the room code provided by the creator and click "Join a Room."

Chatrooms are automatically deleted after a period of inactivity to keep the platform clean and efficient.

Chatrooms are automatically deleted when all users leave or after a period of inactivity. There is no manual delete option.

Yes, you can edit or delete your messages in real time. Changes will be reflected across all connected users.

When you leave a chatroom, your messages remain in the chat history unless the chatroom is deleted.

Account Questions

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling out the required details.

Yes, registered users can modify their username and email from the "Modify Account" page.

Currently, Hermius does not support password recovery. Please contact support for assistance.

You can delete your account from the "Modify Account" page. This will also delete all your messages and data.
